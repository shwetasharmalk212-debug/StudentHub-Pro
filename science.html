🔬 Class 10 Science Notes

Chapter-wise short notes for quick revision.

⚡ Chapter 1: Chemical Reactions and Equations

  • Chemical reaction: Process where substances change into new substances
  • Balanced equation: Same number of atoms on both sides
  • Types: Combination, decomposition, displacement, double displacement
  • Oxidation: Gain of oxygen / loss of hydrogen

🧪 Chapter 2: Acids, Bases and Salts

  • Acids: Sour taste (HCl, H₂SO₄)
  • Bases: Bitter, soapy (NaOH, KOH)
  • pH scale: 0 to 14
  • Acid + Base → Salt + Water (Neutralization)

💡 Chapter 3: Light

  • Light travels in straight line
  • Reflection law: angle of incidence = angle of reflection
  • Mirror types: Concave and Convex
  • Lens: Convex (converging), Concave (diverging)

⚡ Chapter 4: Electricity

  • Ohm’s Law: V = IR
  • Resistance depends on length, area, material
  • Series: Current same
  • Parallel: Voltage same

🌱 Chapter 5: Life Processes

  • Nutrition, respiration, transport, excretion
  • Photosynthesis: CO₂ + H₂O → Glucose + O₂
  • Respiration releases energy

🌿 Chapter 6: Control and Coordination

  • Brain + nervous system control body
  • Hormones control growth and functions
  • Reflex actions are automatic responses

🧬 Chapter 7: Heredity

  • Traits are passed from parents to offspring
  • Genes control characteristics
  • Mendel is father of genetics
