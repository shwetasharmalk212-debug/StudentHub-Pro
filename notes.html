📘 Class 10 Maths Chapter 1: Real Numbers

Topic: Euclid’s Division Lemma

If we divide a number a by b, then there exist unique integers q and r such that:

Formula: a = bq + r

Where:

  • a = dividend
  • b = divisor
  • q = quotient
  • r = remainder (0 ≤ r < b)

📌 Euclid’s Division Algorithm

Used to find HCF (Highest Common Factor) of two numbers.

Steps:

  1. Apply Euclid’s division lemma on two numbers
  2. Replace divisor with remainder
  3. Repeat until remainder becomes 0
  4. Last divisor is HCF

📌 Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic

Every composite number can be expressed as a product of primes uniquely (except order).

📌 Irrational Numbers

Numbers that cannot be written in p/q form are irrational.

Examples: √2, √3, π

⭐ Important Point

HCF × LCM = Product of two numbers
